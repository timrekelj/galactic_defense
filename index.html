How to play

The goal of the game is to defend your base from the incoming waves of enemies. You can do that by placing towers on the map. Towers will automatically attack enemies that are in their range.

You can place towers my pressing Enter. Tower will be placed on chosen tile. You can choose different tiles by pressing the arrow keys.

Each tower costs 100 money. You can get money by killing enemies. Small enemy gives you 50 money and big one gives you 100.

You can also use mouse to move camera around the map and zoom in and out.

If you want to pause the game, press Esc.

The game is over when you:
lose all your lives -> when enemies come to the end of path, or
you win -> you place tower on every tower tile.
